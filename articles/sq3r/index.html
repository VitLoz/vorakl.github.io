A reading comprehension method

The SQ3R is a reading comprehension method of reading textbook materials is named for its five steps: Survey, Question, Read, Recite, and Review. It offers an active and fairly efficient approach to getting information stored as a text. A text form is far from the ideal or best source of meaningful data for our minds, but the SQ3R method makes it possible to remember, understand, and integrate new knowledge with what we already know.
